Featured Projects

Campus Codefest released summer 2014

In 2013, a few University developers and I created a blended unconference and hackathon we called "Campus Codefest" (CCF). The tools available were tolerable to plan the event, but missing a few key features. The CCF app blends idea generation, voting, volunteering and event registration. The application can be used by any organization, but currently the primary organization using it is the University of Minnesota.

FleaQuest released fall 2013

FleaQuest is a welcoming community where all may contribute by adding listings and sharing knowledge to grow the site for the greater good of the vintage and antiques community. Developed in Ruby on Rails. I was the primary developer on this project.
