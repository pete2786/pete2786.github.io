Featured Projects

CoEventer Personal/Open Source. Released summer 2014

CoEventer is a collaborative, idea driven event planning tool. Unconferences, hackathons and other events with community driven agendas require some upfront work. CoEventer helps you from start to finish. I originally developed this app to help with the University of Minnesota Campus Codefest event. It has recently been adopted by [Open Twin Cities](http://www.opentwincities.org/) to help run future hackathons.

Team Management Sport Ngin. Released fall 2015

The (Team Management)[http://www.sportngin.com/team/] was a major initiative for my team during 2015. I primarily developed the API but also contributed front end work on the project.

FleaQuest Contract. Released fall 2013

FleaQuest is a welcoming community where all may contribute by adding listings and sharing knowledge to grow the site for the greater good of the vintage and antiques community. Developed in Ruby on Rails. I was the primary developer on this project.
